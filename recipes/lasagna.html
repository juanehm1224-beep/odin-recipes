Delicious Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add minced garlic, salt, pepper, and dried basil. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with half of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with 4 lasagna noodles, half of the ricotta mixture, half of the cooked ground beef, and a third of the remaining marinara sauce. Repeat layers once more, then top with remaining noodles, marinara sauce, and shredded mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
